## My Philosophy

Most AI projects fail. Not because the models are wrong — because nobody asked the right business question first.

I've watched enterprises spend millions on AI initiatives that should have been a SQL query. I've seen teams burn months building science projects — impressive tech that solves no real problem. Data scientists and technologists love building toys. I get it. But the graveyard of failed AI projects is full of beautiful models that nobody asked for.

I work the other way. What's the actual problem? What's causing friction? What would move the needle? Answer those first. Then pick your stack.

I bring a structured methodology to every engagement — from fuzzy idea to enterprise solution, I'm involved at every step. Whiteboard, architecture, data, models, deployment, iteration. That's what my clients are paying for, and it's not something I publish. But the philosophy underneath it is simple: rigorous process beats clever algorithms every time.
