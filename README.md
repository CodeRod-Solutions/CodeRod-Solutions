## üí° My Approach

**Understand the business problem first.** Then map the technology to solve it. Not the other way around.

Everyone wants "AI" right now. But most problems don't need AI - they need smart engineering. A well-designed database query can solve what people think requires machine learning. That's where a lot of organizations struggle. They get caught up in the hype cycle and miss the fundamentals. What's the actual problem? What's causing friction? What would move the needle?

Answer those questions first. Then pick your stack.
